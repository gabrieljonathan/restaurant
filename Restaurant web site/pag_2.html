Main Menu



Starters


Bloomin' Onion

Our famous giant, golden onions with authentic flavour. Accompanied by our wonderful Bloom sauce.


Aussie Cheese Fries

Our fries with an irresistible topping with a mix of cheeses and bacon. The final touch is the Ranch sauce. Turn your potato into a veggie by asking for no bacon.


(Veggie) Jack Nachos

Crispy corn tortillas served with black beans, cherry tomatoes, purple cabbage, mixed cheeses, jalapeño slices and vegetarian meat (shredded jackfruit prepared with Bold Flavour).


Mains


Steak N' Chips

Our filet mignon strips and breaded. Crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, accompanied by our Smoked Mayo Mayo and a side dish.


Ribs Double Decker

Burger with our juicy shredded ribs, neat slices of emmenthal cheese and finished with Killer sauce on a brioche bun.


(Veggie) Blue Cheese Burguer

Vegetarian burger made of broccoli, cauliflower and gorgonzola cheese, topped with a mix of melted cheeses. Served with gorgonzola and smoked mayo sauces, lettuce, tomato and pickled cucumber on brioche bread.


Desserts


Cinnamon Oblivion

The dessert that our waiters love to explain. Contains nuts or nut derivative.


Thunder Trio

A trio of miniature thunders, bringing together all the bold flavour that only Outback has: Chocolate Thunder, Havanna Thunder and Hazelnut Thunder.


Havanna Thunder

Our brownie, with pecans, now in a Havanna dulce de leche version. Served with vanilla ice cream, whipped cream, biscuit crumble and finished with a delicious Havanna dulce de leche syrup.
